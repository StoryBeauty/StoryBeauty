How It Works – Easy Online Booking

All bookings are managed through Booksy.com, a trusted third-party scheduling platform. Booking your appointment is simple and hassle-free:

1️⃣ Select Your Service – Browse through the list of treatments and choose the one that suits your needs.

2️⃣ Redirect to Booksy – Once you select a service, you’ll be taken to Booksy webpage.

3️⃣ Pick a Time Slot – Choose a date and time that works for you from the available slots in the calendar.

4️⃣ Confirm & Book – Follow the steps on Booksy to finalize your appointment.

That’s it! Your booking is instantly confirmed, and you’ll receive all the necessary details.
